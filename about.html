About Me

Hello, I'm Sameer Jaiswal

I'm a passionate Game Designer & Developer from Kolkata, India. I recently graduated with a B.Sc. in Gaming Design from Sharda University, where I spent the last 3 years building games from scratch using Unity (2D & 3D), Autodesk Maya, Substance Painter, and more.

I've created multiple solo projects where I handled everything from 3D modeling, rigging, and animation to gameplay programming, UI/UX design, and sound integration. I also have foundational knowledge in game AI systems in Unity, which I explored through prototypes and book-based learning. While Unity is my core engine, I'm currently preparing to learn Unreal Engine 5 to expand my creative and technical abilities further.
